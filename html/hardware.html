There are lots of computer parts, however we will go through the top 4 parts in considerations of choosing your laptop.

Click here to Download This Page, for future referrence.

  1. Processor
    • Brain of the Computer
    • Usally refers to i3, i5 or i7
      • The higher the number, the faster your laptop can multi-task and run task.

  2. RAM
    • A temporary memory your computer used to perform basic tasks.
    • The higher the amount, the more your laptop can multitask and load large amount of softwares for your work.

  3. Storage (SSD / HDD)
    • A storage drive to keep your files in your Laptop.
    • The larger the storage size, more files you will be able to store in your laptop.
    • There are 2 kinds of storage drive avaliable in the market right now;
      • HDD: A storage device with moving mechanism, which will take longer time to find your files.
      • SSD: A storage device with no moving parts, which is much faster and compact.

  4. Graphics Card
    • Gamers you were waiting to read until this, aren't you?
    • A seperated processor that are found in the high end tier of laptops.
    • It's used to get fast rendering and smooth gameplay.
